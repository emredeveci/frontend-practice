Welcome to Svelte Nursery

Welcome to Svelte Nursery, your premier destination for all things green and growing! Nestled in the heart of the community, our nursery is more than just a place to buy plants – it's a sanctuary for plant lovers and gardening enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting your journey with plants, you'll find an abundance of high-quality, healthy plants to suit every need and preference. From vibrant flowering plants to lush indoor greenery and hardy outdoor shrubs, our extensive selection promises to inspire and delight. Our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to offer expert advice, ensuring you find the perfect additions to your garden and home.

At Svelte Nursery, we pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship. Our plants are cultivated with care, using eco-friendly practices that promote healthy growth while protecting our natural resources. We also offer a range of garden supplies, from organic fertilizers to eco-conscious pest control solutions, helping you nurture your garden sustainably. Join us for one of our popular gardening workshops or community events, where you can learn new skills and connect with fellow plant enthusiasts. At Green Haven Nursery, we're dedicated to helping you grow a greener world, one plant at a time.

We do plenty of "planty" services for you:

  • Plant Sales and Delivery
  • Garden Design Consultation
  • Gardening Workshops and Classes
  • Custom Potting and Replanting Services
  • Seasonal Planting Services
  • Pest and Disease Management
