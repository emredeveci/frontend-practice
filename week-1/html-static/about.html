About Us

The Story Behind Svelte Nursery

Svelte Nursery began as a dream in the heart of our founder, Emily Green, who has always been passionate about plants and gardening. Emily's love for nature was cultivated during her childhood, spending countless hours in her grandmother's lush garden, learning the secrets of nurturing plants. After years of working in various horticultural roles and gaining a wealth of experience, Emily decided to turn her lifelong dream into reality. In the spring of 2005, Svelte Nursery opened its doors, nestled in a quaint corner of the city, with a mission to bring the joy of gardening to the community.

From its humble beginnings, Svelte Nursery quickly became known for its wide variety of high-quality plants and exceptional customer service. Emily's dedication to sustainability and eco-friendly practices set the nursery apart, making it a haven for environmentally conscious gardeners. As word spread about the unique offerings and the knowledgeable staff, Svelte Nursery grew in popularity, attracting plant enthusiasts from all over the city and beyond.

An Iconic Part of the City

Over the years, Svelte Nursery has blossomed into an iconic part of the city, beloved by locals and visitors alike. Our commitment to excellence and community engagement has been at the heart of this growth. We host a variety of workshops and events, from beginner gardening classes to advanced landscaping seminars, fostering a sense of community and shared learning. Our seasonal festivals have become a highlight of the year, bringing people together to celebrate the beauty of nature.

Svelte Nursery is more than just a place to buy plants; it's a sanctuary where people come to find peace, inspiration, and a connection to the natural world. Our team, many of whom have been with us since the beginning, are passionate about helping you create your own green oasis, offering expert advice and personalized service.

As we look to the future, Svelte Nursery remains dedicated to our founding principles of sustainability, education, and community. We are proud to be a part of the city's rich tapestry and grateful for the support and loyalty of our customers. Together, we continue to grow and thrive, nurturing a greener, more beautiful world for generations to come.

Milestones of our nursery:

  1. 2005: Svelte Nursery founded by Emre Deveci in the heart of the city.
  2. 2008: Introduction of eco-friendly gardening practices and sustainability initiatives.
  3. 2010: First annual Spring Garden Festival hosted at Svelte Nursery.
  4. 2012: Expansion of nursery facilities to accommodate growing demand.
  5. 2015: Launch of online store for plant sales and delivery services.
  6. 2017: Collaboration with local schools to promote youth gardening programs.
  7. 2020: Adoption of advanced greenhouse technologies for year-round plant cultivation.
  8. 2022: Recognition as "Best Plant Nursery" in the city by local gardening association.
